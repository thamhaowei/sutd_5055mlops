Overview
SUTD adopts a cohort-based fixed fee model whereby the tuition fee for a cohort intake will remain unchanged throughout the normal candidature of students. However, tuition fees for every new cohort are subject to change from year to year.
With the MOE Tuition Grant provided by the Government of Singapore, eligible students will get to enjoy subsidised tuition fees.
Besides tuition fees, other educational expenses include compulsory miscellaneous fees and housing fees. The annual compulsory miscellaneous fees cover registration to student council, students’ activities, insurance, sports facilities, IT facilities and academic related services.
